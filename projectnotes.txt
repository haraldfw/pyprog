The alpine-club runs an alpine park with lifts and tracks. The guests can rent skiing-equipment, these are complete packs with skis, boots and ski-poles.

There are 5 different types of packs to choose between, and the guests should be able to rent the packs with price per hour, day and week.

The guests shall also have the ability to rent lift-cards. There are 3 different types of these, day-, week- and season-cards.
Adults and children have different prices.

The website must contain an overview of the different ski-packs and their respective prices. There has to be a small description for each pack.
